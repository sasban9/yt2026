// Daily Prompts

Questions that reveal who your child really is.

Not random questions. Carefully designed behavioral prompts that build a picture over time.

💡 What made you curious today? Was there something you couldn't stop thinking about?
⚡ What task drained your energy? What did you find repetitive or boring?
📺 What YouTube topic did you binge? Your internet behavior reveals hidden strengths.
🔧 Did you enjoy solving, explaining, organizing, or designing today? Which felt most natural?
⏱️ What activity made time disappear? What were you doing when you lost track of the clock?
🌟 What kind of people do you admire? What qualities in others do you want to develop?
😤 What frustrated you today? Often, frustration reveals what you deeply care about.
